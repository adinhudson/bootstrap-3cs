OUR STORY

  • December 2018

    3CS Launches New Website for Kia Motors Sri Lanka

    The website was designed in a modern user friendly style and layout. It’s an interactive site, which gives visitors useful information about the products, services and facilities available at KIA Motors Sri Lanka. The site is fully responsive and compatible with all mobile devices and platforms. As with every 3CS solution, this website runs on state of the art infrastructure, and has been highly optimized for search engines.

  • December 2018

    3CS Wins Best Finance Website Award for LOLC Finance Website

    LOLC Finance PLC (www.lolcfinance.com) was awarded the Best Website in the Finance category at the SLT ZERO ONE Digital Excellence Awards 2018. The website was designed by 3CS (www.3cs.lk). The award ceremony was held at the Galadari Hotel on 11th December 2018. 3CS also won two awards as Best Digital Media Agency in the Finance and Travel categories.

  • July 2018

    3CS Wins Best Web Developer Award for 7th Consecutive Time

    3CS won a Best Web Developer award at Bestweb for a record 7th consecutive time. The News1st website designed and managed by 3CS won the Gold Award in the Media, Sports & Entertainment category, and also won the Gold Award for Best Overall Website in Sri Lanka. 3CS designed websites for Renuka City Hotel, John Keells Foundation and Ceylon Escapes also won awards in other categories.

  • July 2018

    News 1st Wins Gold Medal for Best Media Website

    The website is designed with a superior user experience (UX) as the key objective. Over the medium term we aim to increase visitor traffic to the site, increase global reach, improve engagement and improve interaction with users of the site. The site is also closely integrated with social media channels. It is fully responsive and compatible with all mobile devices and platforms.

  • May 2018

    3CS Launches New Website for LOLC Finance

    The redesign of the LOLC Finance website was timed to coincide with the merger of LOLC Finance with LOLC Micro Credit. With a focus on user-friendliness, the new LOLC Finance site is simple and easy to navigate. It is mobile responsive and compatible with all devices, browsers and platforms. The design 3CS presented for LOLC Finance reflects our evolving design language and philosophy. The site offers many interactive features including a branch locator, rate calculators and access to online portals and the mobile app.
